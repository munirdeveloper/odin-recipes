Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Tomatoes/Tomato Paste
  • Fresh chicken
  • The seasonings of your choice
  • Garlic, peppers
  • Heavy cream

Steps

  1. Fry your onions, chop your tomatoes
  2. Add your chicken and stir
  3. Add your seasonings, garlic, peppers and stir
  4. Add heavy cream and stir
  5. Let it sit in medium heat for half an hour
